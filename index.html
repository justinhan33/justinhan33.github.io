California Actuarial League is a pre-professional organization that provides resources for students interested in becoming actuaries. For over twenty years Cal Actuarial League has partnered with companies such as Aon, Beam Dental, and Mercer as well as connected UC Berkeleyâ€™s business & data analysts to the industry. Philanthropy

For the past three years, I have been a member and advocate of AADP. AADP, or Asian American Donor Program, is an official recruitment center of the Be The Match registry with the primary mission of increasing the availability of potential stem cells donors for patients with life threatening diseases curable by a stem cell transplant.

As a member of AADP, I along with several others, regularly help spread awareness on campus by giving talks, tabling, or even posting on social media. Over the last few years, we successfully recruited hundreds of new members to join the registry and the AADP following has been steadily growing.
