California Actuarial League is a pre-professional organization that provides resources for students interested in becoming actuaries. For over twenty years Cal Actuarial League has partnered with companies such as Aon, Beam Dental, and Mercer as well as connected UC Berkeleyâ€™s business & data analysts to the industry. From professional development to social engagement, UC Berkeley's Actuarial Club allows students, like myself, to get a better understanding of the acturial field while sharpening any soft skills necessary for sucess regardless of industry.

As a member of the club, I was fortunate to partake in the Ninth Annual Actuarial Case Competition. Over the course of three months, my team and I worked diligently on cases provided by renowned consulting and insurance companies, meeting regularly, deliberating, and executing. I was able to get a tremendous amount of hands on experience with real world data, something that the classrooms simply lacked. I learned how to lead a team, effectively manage multiple tasks, and deliver quality work under time pressure. In the end, we managed to qualify for the final round.
