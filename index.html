About Me

A Brief Introduction

Born and raised in the Bay Area, I am currently a senior at the University of California, Berkeley studying Statistics with a focus in Computer Science and Economics. I am particularly interested in statistical learning and predictive modeling. I hope to leverage such tools to extract insightful stories behind raw data in order to help businesses grow and achieve their goals. Outside of statistics, I enjoy playing competitive basketball and working out as well as staying up to date with the latest trends in fashion, food, and music!
